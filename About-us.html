Julia's SPEECH

Our Story

Our founder Abby is the granddaughter, and daughter to immigrants from the Soviet Union who have faced tremendous struggle with learning English while struggling to provide for their family. She has heard countless stories of her grandmother Julia expressing how she had to stay up late watching movies writing down words, doing all she can to learn the language, so her family had a chance in America. Our founder believes no one should have to feel the pressures of everyday life, changes, immigration, etc. while worrying about English, so through a strong team of dedicated coders we were able to bring to the public "Julia's SPEECH", a website designed to be your main toolbox for learning English, because we believe no one should fear their English skills, but rather be empowered by it.
